french toast

Description

French toast, a beloved breakfast classic, transforms simple ingredients into a decadent delight. Thick slices of bread are soaked in a mixture of beaten eggs, milk, and a hint of cinnamon, infusing them with rich flavor and a custard-like texture. Cooked to golden perfection on a griddle or skillet, French toast emerges with a crisp exterior and a soft, pillowy center. Its versatility shines as it can be enjoyed simply with a drizzle of maple syrup or adorned with a myriad of toppings such as fresh fruit, powdered sugar, or a dollop of whipped cream. Whether served as a leisurely weekend brunch or a quick weekday indulgence, French toast never fails to comfort and satisfy with its warm, comforting embrace.

ingredients

  • Slices of bread (such as white, whole wheat, brioche, challah, or French bread)
  • Eggs
  • Milk (or alternative milk such as almond milk, soy milk, or coconut milk)
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • Salt
  • Butter or oil (for frying)
  • Optional toppings
  • Maple syrup
  • Powdered sugar

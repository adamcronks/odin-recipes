pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a breakfast staple enjoyed worldwide for their simplicity and versatility. Made from a basic batter of flour, eggs, milk, and a touch of sugar and baking powder, pancakes are cooked on a griddle or skillet until golden brown and fluffy. Their appeal lies in their ability to be customized with a variety of toppings, from classic maple syrup and butter to fruit, chocolate chips, or savory options like bacon and cheese. Whether served as a morning treat or a midnight snack, pancakes never fail to delight with their comforting aroma and delicious taste.

ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk (you can use any type of milk - dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
  • teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Optional toppings
  • Maple syrup
  • Fresh fruit
  • Whipped cream
